# 掌海科技科普文摘（十）——Vorticity, gyroscopic precession, and spin-curvature Force - 知乎

**FROM：Wei Chieh Liang and Si Chen Leey October 2012 Physical review D: Particles and fields 87(4) DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.87.044024**
