1. When one partner is made hateful to the other.
2. When there is a physical separation of the partners.
3. When the emission of semen is prevented.
4. When the semen is infertile.
5. When a man’s penis remains flabby.
6. When natural drugs prevent conception.
7. When the female genitals contract or the male organ retracts.
